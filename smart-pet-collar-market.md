<p><ul><li>GPS Based</li><li>Radio Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart pet collar market can be categorized into GPS-based and radio-based types. GPS-based collars utilize satellite technology to track a pet's location, providing real-time updates and enabling owners to monitor their pets over long distances. In contrast, radio-based collars use short-range radio signals to communicate between the collar and a handheld receiver, which is ideal for local tracking in areas like parks or yards. Both types enhance pet safety and provide peace of mind for pet owners.</p></p>
