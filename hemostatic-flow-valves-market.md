<p><p>The hemostatic flow valves market is categorized into several types based on design and functionality. One-handed valves allow for easy operation with one hand, enhancing efficiency during procedures. Sideport tubing valves feature an additional port for fluid management, enabling simultaneous fluid access. Straight connector valves provide a direct connection for streamlined flow and are typically used in various surgical settings. Y connector valves allow dual flow paths, facilitating the management of multiple fluids or instruments in complex procedures.</p></p>
