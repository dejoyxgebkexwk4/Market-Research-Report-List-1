<p><p>The conductive silicone rubber market is divided into two primary types: thermally conductive and electrically conductive silicone rubber. Thermally conductive silicone rubber efficiently dissipates heat, making it ideal for applications in electronics and automotive industries, where managing temperature is crucial. Conversely, electrically conductive silicone rubber allows controlled flow of electricity, used in components like gaskets and seals to prevent electromagnetic interference. Both types are valued for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, enabling diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
